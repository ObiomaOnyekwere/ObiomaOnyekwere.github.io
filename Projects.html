Comatosis

Comatosis is a five nights at Freddy's clone type of game created using the godot game engine. It was published on itch.io on 09 May , 2023. Last updated around December, the game hosts: 5 main game nights , a custom night mode, secret minigames and even a full minigame contained within the extras menu. Artwork was created by a friend of mine but other than that, audio and code was all by me.
